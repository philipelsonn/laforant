 What is LaForant? 

 

LaForant is our take on the classic team based first-person shooter set in the near future.  Two teams battle it out to see who gets on top, across various maps and gamemodes.  Players play as one of many agents, each having unique abilities that can assist them during combat.  Team members need to communicate and work together if they want to win. 

 AGENTS 

Choose between a wide range of agents, each with different skillsets and playstyles that can assist in your gunplay.  Master all agents so you can be the one that dominates the battlefield. 

 MAPS 

EXPLORE UNIQUE PLACES

Travel across different maps from different regions and strategize your way through victory.  Communicate with your team members to increase your chance of winning.
